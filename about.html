About

GITAM is a Category-I Deemed to be University with campuses in Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru. Founded in 1980, it offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including engineering, science, management, pharmacy, architecture, and law. The university emphasizes a dynamic, application-oriented education system and a culture of kindness, aiming to develop skilled professionals and encourage impactful research.
